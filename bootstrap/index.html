Based in the Northern Virginia metropolitan area, Crew 612 is a philanthropy brotherhood that focuses on giving back to the community through participating in fundraisers and charity events.
Besides helping out the community, being in this group of excellent young men means having friends for life.
It is all about representing the highest standard of excellence and helping others.

We welcome people from all walks of life.

We provide you with an extended family and a brotherhood for life.
